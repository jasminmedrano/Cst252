Purpose

The purpose of lab 4 is to get in the habit of thinking in terms of pseudocoding before sarting on coding.

Results

These are our two lists that we came up with. The first one is a get ready with me and the second one is how to manage files/folders.

Task 1: Pseudocode an everyday task: Get Ready With Me!
  1. Wake up
  2. Get out of bed
  3. Go brush teeth with toothpaste and water
  4. Go take a shower
  5. Change into clean clothes
  6. Make yourself a morning breakfast
  7. Have breakfast
  8. Clean dishes
Task 2: Pseudocode a computer task: Managing Files/folders on your computer!
  1. Go through all the files that are outdated or unnecessary
  2. Free storage by trashing unnecessary files/folders
  3. Name files by following the naming conventions
  4. Create folders or designate a place for all of the files
  5. Organize and order files that are from most important to least
  6. Back up any files
  7. Save files and put them in the right place when you first create them for easy access
